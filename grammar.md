title: Grammar
---

A sentence:

`[reference][PRT_REF] [subject][PRT_SUB] [direct object][PRT_OBJ] [verb] [sentence final particle]`

or

`[subject][PRT_SUB] [reference][PRT_REF] [direct object][PRT_OBJ] [verb] [sentence final particle]`

Any word can be prefixed with a statement:

`[sentence][PRT_MOD] [word]`
